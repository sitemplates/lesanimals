Client

The Observatoire des Transitions Professionnelles conducts an annual survey to identify the French trends of employees who have decided to change jobs.

In depth

  • Since 2021
  • +280 000 data
  • Public service

Need

Provide access to a large volume of data to support advisors and employees in their career transition projects.

Solution

Creation of a visualization tool of thousands of data powered by the Google API allowing to filter the results according to its own criteria.

Today, thousands of professionals use the tool to refine their career transition plans and better target the jobs that are recruiting.
